## Block 1 — British Shadowing (15 min) — NON-NEGOTIABLE

**Source:** Cambridge University YouTube — search "Cambridge lecture biology"

**Week 5 Phonology Focus: Connected Speech — Linking**

1. Linking drill: identify every word-final consonant followed by a vowel-initial word (e.g., "an_argument", "focus_on"). Shadow those phrases 3 times each.
2. Intrusive /r/ drill: notice where speakers insert /r/ between vowels (e.g., "the idea(r) of", "law(r) and order"). Imitate without over-correcting.
3. Consonant cluster simplification: find a phrase with clusters like /nts/ or /sts/ (e.g., "scientists", "tests"). Shadow slowly, then at full speed until the reduction sounds natural.

**Protocol (condensed for Phase 3):**
1. Listen once (2 min)
2. Shadow at full speed — no slow speed in Phase 3 (8 min)
3. Record 30 seconds. Check linking accuracy — are you connecting word boundaries smoothly? (5 min)
