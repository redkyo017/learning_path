## Block 2 — Speaking Template Review (20 min) — ONLY if Mock 3 < 90

Say the opening sentence of each template aloud from memory:
- Task 1: "I believe that ___ for two main reasons. First, ___."
- Task 2: "The announcement states that ___. The man/woman [agrees/disagrees] with this [change/proposal]."
- Task 3: "According to the reading, ___ refers to ___. The professor illustrates this by discussing ___."
- Task 4: "In the lecture, the professor discusses ___ by providing ___ examples. First, ___."

Record each. Check: British RP intonation, no rising tone on statements, final consonants clear.
