# Day 27 — Integrated Sprint: Weekend Listening Mock + AWL Review + Extended Shadowing

**Phase:** 3 — Integrated Practice | **Time:** 4 hours weekend | **Magoosh: 40 cards**

---

## Block 1 — British Shadowing (15 min) — NON-NEGOTIABLE

**Source:** BBC Radio 4 In Our Time — search "In Our Time history episode" and pick any history episode

**Week 5 Phonology Focus: Connected Speech — Consolidation**

1. Full connected-speech review: choose a 30-second segment from the episode. Identify at least one example each of linking, elision, and assimilation. Label them in your notes.
2. Speed and rhythm: shadow the same segment at full speed 3 times without pausing. Focus on the rhythmic beat falling on stressed syllables.
3. Self-correction drill: record yourself, play it back, find the one word or phrase where your L1 habits still show (Vietnamese-origin flat intonation, final consonant dropping, etc.), and re-shadow that phrase 5 more times.

**Protocol (condensed for Phase 3):**
1. Listen once (2 min)
2. Shadow at full speed — no slow speed in Phase 3 (8 min)
3. Record 30 seconds. Check all three connected-speech features are present (5 min)
