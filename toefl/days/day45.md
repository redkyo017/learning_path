I have practised every day for 44 days. I am ready."

---

## During the Test

### Reading
- Skim first: read title + first sentence of each paragraph only (2 min per passage)
- Identify thesis + 3 supporting arguments before answering questions
- Do not re-read full passage to answer questions — skim and locate

### Listening
- Take structured notes: main argument + 2-3 supporting details
- Do not try to write everything — capture structure, not every word
- Listen for signpost phrases: "however", "in contrast", "for example", "the key point is"

### Speaking
- Template opening in your very first sentence — do not improvise the opening
- Speak toward the back of the room: projects your voice and slows your pace naturally
- British RP delivery: stress CONTENT words, reduce function words
- Do NOT rush — 60 seconds is enough time for Tasks 2-4

### Writing
- Task 1: Cover all 3 lecture counterpoints (even if briefly for the third)
- Task 2: State your position in sentence 1. Give two specific reasons. Write 120+ words.
- Use AWL collocations naturally — do not force every one in
