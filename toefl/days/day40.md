## Block 2 — Targeted Section Repair (90 min)

Work ONLY on the section that scored lowest in Mock 2 (from progress_tracker.md).
Use the same category-drilling approach as Day 37.

**If Reading was lowest:**
Do 2 full timed reading passages. Categorise every wrong answer after each.
Focus only on the error category that appears most often.

**If Listening was lowest:**
Do 3 note-taking drills with academic lectures.
After each: compare your notes to the transcript — identify what you consistently miss.

**If Speaking was lowest:**
Record 4 repetitions of your weakest task type with different prompts.
Compare to a Score 4 example on TST Prep for the same task type.

**If Writing was lowest:**
Do 2 Task 1 responses on different topics (20 min each).
Read both against the Task 1 rubric — score yourself on coverage, language, organisation.

## Block 3 — AWL Collocation Review (25 min)
