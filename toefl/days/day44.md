# Day 44 — Rest & Logistics

**Phase:** Final | **Time:** 30 minutes maximum

---

## Today's Only Tasks

### 1. Confirm Test Logistics (10 min)

- [ ] Test centre address confirmed — know exactly where to go
- [ ] Arrival time planned: 30 minutes before test start
- [ ] ID document confirmed: passport or national ID
- [ ] Test confirmation email/number saved on phone
- [ ] Understand what NOT to bring: notes, unauthorised devices, food (check test centre rules)

### 2. Light Template Review (10 min)

Read ONLY the opening sentence of each Speaking template from templates/speaking_templates.md.
Do not drill. Do not study. Just read them once.

### 3. Physical Preparation (10 min)

- Plan tomorrow's meals: normal breakfast, nothing heavy, nothing new
- Set two alarms for tomorrow morning
- Aim to be in bed by 10pm

---

## Do NOT Do Today

- No Magoosh
- No listening practice
- No mock tests
- No new vocabulary
- No reading TOEFL passages

The brain consolidates learning during rest. Studying today actively reduces test performance tomorrow.
