## Block 1 — British Shadowing (20 min)

**Source:** BBC 6 Minute English "health" — search this on YouTube or at bbc.co.uk/learningenglish

**Week 1 Phonology Focus: Non-rhotic vowels + final consonant clusters**
Specifically drill:
- Non-rhotic "or": "more" → /mɔː/, "before" → /bɪfɔː/, "doctor" → /dɒktə/ — no r at the end
- Non-rhotic "air": "there" → /ðɛə/, "care" → /kɛə/, "aware" → /əwɛə/ — glide to schwa
- Final clusters: "texts" → /teksts/, "months" → /mʌnθs/, "health" → /helθ/ — hold each consonant

**Protocol:**
1. Listen once, no transcript (2 min)
2. Listen again with transcript (2 min)
3. Shadow at 0.75x speed on YouTube (5 min)
4. Shadow at full speed (5 min)
5. Record 30 seconds of yourself. Play back. Note: are you dropping the r at the end of words, and are your "air" glides smooth rather than rhotic? (6 min)

## Block 2 — Speaking: Task 2 Campus Announcement (30 min)

**Template:** templates/speaking_templates.md → Task 2

**Prompt:** Campus library hours change — the university is extending library hours to midnight on weekdays starting next semester (TST Prep style). Read the announcement for 30 seconds, then listen to two students discuss it for 1 minute, then respond.

**Steps:**
1. Read the Task 2 template (2 min)
2. Prep: 30 seconds — note the change + student's opinion + one reason from conversation
3. Record response (60 seconds) — use the template opening: "According to the announcement…"
4. Play back. Score yourself:
   - Delivery (1-4): ___
   - Language (1-4): ___
   - Development (1-4): ___
5. Identify ONE fix (e.g., missing the student's second reason, running over time, monotone delivery)
6. Record again applying that fix

## Block 3 — Reading: Academic Skimming (25 min)

**Topic:** Psychology/behaviour

Search "TOEFL reading practice psychology behaviour passage" for a 700-word practice passage.

**Skimming protocol:**
1. Read title only → predict: what is this about? (30 sec)
2. Read ONLY the first sentence of each paragraph (2 min)
3. Write: thesis = ___, main arguments = (1) ___ (2) ___ (3) ___
4. Answer the questions WITHOUT re-reading (15 min)
5. Review wrong answers: A=vocab B=inference C=main idea D=detail E=rhetorical purpose (5 min)
