## Block 1 — British Shadowing (20 min)

**Source:** BBC 6 Minute English "money finance" — search this on YouTube or at bbc.co.uk/learningenglish

**Week 1 Phonology Focus: Non-rhotic vowels + final consonant clusters**
Specifically drill:
- Non-rhotic "ear/eer": "hear" → /hɪə/, "career" → /kərɪə/ → in RP: /kəˈrɪə/, "year" → /jɪə/ — no r, vowel glides to schwa
- Long open vowel: "bank" → /bæŋk/, "market" → /mɑːkɪt/, "part" → /pɑːt/ — notice market has the long /ɑː/
- Final clusters with -nds/-nts: "funds" → /fʌndz/, "accounts" → /əkaʊnts/, "contracts" → /kɒntræks/ — do not drop the final s

**Protocol:**
1. Listen once, no transcript (2 min)
2. Listen again with transcript (2 min)
3. Shadow at 0.75x speed on YouTube (5 min)
4. Shadow at full speed (5 min)
5. Record 30 seconds of yourself. Play back. Note: are your non-rhotic ear/eer glides smooth, and are your final -nds and -nts clusters complete? (6 min)

6. Record again applying that fix

## Block 3 — Reading: Academic Skimming (25 min)

**Topic:** Business/economics

Search "TOEFL reading practice business economics passage" for a 700-word practice passage.

**Skimming protocol:**
1. Read title only → predict: what is this about? (30 sec)
2. Read ONLY the first sentence of each paragraph (2 min)
3. Write: thesis = ___, main arguments = (1) ___ (2) ___ (3) ___
4. Answer the questions WITHOUT re-reading (15 min)
5. Review wrong answers: A=vocab B=inference C=main idea D=detail E=rhetorical purpose (5 min)
