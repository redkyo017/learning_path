# Day 34 — Integrated Sprint: Weekend Listening Mock + AWL Review + Extended Shadowing

**Phase:** 3 — Integrated Practice | **Time:** 4 hours weekend | **Magoosh: 40 cards**

---

## Block 1 — British Shadowing (15 min) — NON-NEGOTIABLE

**Source:** BBC Radio 4 In Our Time — search "In Our Time ethics episode" and pick any ethics episode

**Week 6 Phonology Focus: Intonation Polish — Full Intonation Review**

1. Statement vs. question contrast: take one declarative sentence and one yes/no question from the episode. Shadow both. Record and confirm the fall-rise contrast is clear.
2. Wh-question vs. yes/no question: construct one wh-question (falling) and one yes/no question (rising) using vocabulary from the episode. Shadow both. Record the pair.
3. Emphasis for meaning: take a sentence with a debatable claim from the ethics discussion. Say it three times, moving the nuclear stress to a different word each time. Which placement most accurately reflects the speaker's intended meaning?

**Protocol (condensed for Phase 3):**
1. Listen once (2 min)
2. Shadow at full speed — no slow speed in Phase 3 (8 min)
3. Record 30 seconds. Check statement/question contrast and emphasis placement (5 min)
